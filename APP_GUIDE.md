# YIELDX - Complete App Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started

### How to Use the App

1. **Start on the Home Page**: You'll see the sign-in screen with three options
2. **Choose Your Role**: Select between Farmer, Buyer, or Farm Head
3. **Enter Credentials**: Use any email and password (mock authentication)
4. **Explore Dashboard**: Navigate through tabs to access different features

---

## 👨‍🌾 FARMER PORTAL - Complete Walkthrough

### Tab 1: Home (Climate & Weather)
**What you see:**
- Current weather conditions with temperature
- Humidity, wind speed, visibility, pressure
- 7-day forecast with rainfall predictions
- Your crop status with growth percentages
- Water requirements and pest risk levels
- Weather alerts

**Actions you can take:**
- Review daily weather impact on crops
- Plan irrigation based on forecasts
- Monitor pest risk changes
- Receive weather-based alerts

---

### Tab 2: AI Assistant (Zap Icon)
**Five Sub-Tabs:**

#### a) Chat with AI
- Ask questions about your crops
- Get instant farming advice
- Discuss weather impact
- Receive personalized recommendations

**Example Questions:**
- "When should I water my rice fields?"
- "What's causing yellow spots on my wheat?"
- "When is the best time to harvest corn?"

#### b) Pest Detection
- Click upload area or drag-and-drop crop image
- AI analyzes photo and identifies:
  - Disease/pest name
  - Confidence level (%)
  - Severity (Low/Medium/High)
  - Recommended treatment
  - Prevention strategies

**Use When:**
- You notice unusual plant symptoms
- You see insects on crops
- Leaves show discoloration

#### c) Crop Advice
- Get personalized farming recommendations
- Topics covered:
  - Watering schedules
  - Fertilizer application timing
  - Harvest readiness
  - Seasonal best practices

#### d) Market Insights
- Real-time crop pricing
- Price trends (↑ ↓ →)
- Market demand levels
- Price predictions

**Use to:**
- Decide when to sell crops
- Compare market prices
- Plan planting strategy

#### e) Sustainable Farming
- Learn eco-friendly practices
- Crop rotation benefits
- Water harvesting methods
- Organic composting
- Integrated pest management

**Benefits Include:**
- Cost savings (15-40%)
- Yield improvements (15-20%)
- Better soil health
- Reduced chemical use

---

### Tab 3: Map (Location)
**What you see:**
- Your farm location
- Nearby farmers (🚜 green markers)
- Nearby buyers (🛒 blue markers)
- Distance and directions

**Interact with Markers:**
1. Click any marker to see details
2. For farmers: See their location, crops, contact info
3. For buyers: See urgent crop demands, pricing, urgency level

**Filter Options:**
- Show all locations
- Show farmers only
- Show buyers only

**Urgent Buyer Demands Section:**
- Displays buyers needing crops urgently
- Shows:
  - What crop they need
  - Quantity required
  - Price offered
  - Deadline/urgency
  - Contact button

**Use to:**
- Find nearby buyers for your crops
- Discover surrounding farmers
- See market demand in your region

**Categories:**
- All crops

**Actions:**
- Add to cart

- Rate/review buyers

5. **Community Tips** (Message icon)

**Manage Alerts:**
